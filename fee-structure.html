Our Fees are moderate and affordable.

The College is concerned with :

  1. Admission Forms Fees: To Commence Applicant Enrollment Process.
  2. Tuition Fees: Paid Upon Admission To The College; To Have Access To Lectures. (NOT APPLICABLE IF APPLICANT IS AWARDED SCHOLARSHIP BY THE COLLEGE)
  3. Facility User Fees: To Make Use Of College Facilities ( Computer Labs, Library, Research etc..)

NOTE: ALL COLLEGE FEES ARE TO BE PAID BY A BANK DRAFT IN THE NAME OF "AKIM STATE COLLEGE" or "AKIM STATE UNIVERSITY COLLEGE" TO THE COLLEGE or TO AUTHORIZED PERSONNELS ONLY!!!

The Online Portal is made available to assist students who may not be able to have direct or physical contact with the College for their academic needs.

The Online Portal Provides Students With:

  • Processing Admissions Online
  • Paying Fees Online
  • View Payment Ledger & Scheme
  • Register Courses
  • Drop Courses
  • View Results
  • Provide Feedbacks and
  • Profile Evaluation
The Online Portal is made available by a PRIVATE INDIVIDUAL who has very little support. For that reason to keep serving the student body, the usage of the system comes at a cost.

To use the System, Students will have to pay GH¢20 per semester.

STUDENTS AND PEOPLE ARE WELCOMED TO DONATE TO THE SYSTEM!!!

These payments are gathered to improve and maintain the system for security, development and server configurations.
